1 Overview

This blueprint maps the core of the LeanFlagAlgebras library — the FlagAlgebra/ directory — which formalizes Razborov’s flag algebras in Lean 4. The development is layered, and each chapter below corresponds to one layer:

  • Flags (Chapter 2): labeled graphs, their isomorphisms, and the flag \(\mathrm{Flag}\, \sigma \) as an isomorphism class.

  • Subflag densities (Chapter 3): how often one flag occurs inside another, and the chain rules relating single, pair, and triple densities.

  • The flag algebra \(\mathcal{A}^\sigma \) (Chapter 4): the commutative \(\mathbb {R}\)-algebra obtained by quotienting formal flag combinations by Razborov’s averaging relations, with multiplication defined through pair densities.

  • The unlabeling operator (Chapter 5): \([\! [\, \cdot \, ]\! ]_0 : \mathcal{A}^\sigma \to \mathcal{A}^\emptyset \), which averages away the labels.

  • Positivity and SOS (Chapter 6): positive homomorphisms, the semantic order \(\le \) in which density bounds are stated, and the sum-of-squares certificates that prove them.

  • Limit semantics (Chapter 7): the analytic side (Razborov’s Theorems 3.3 and 3.5) identifying positive homomorphisms with limits of subgraph densities, and the resulting Cauchy–Schwarz inequality.

  • Application (Chapter 9): Mantel’s theorem, the Turán density of the triangle, proved with the machinery above.
